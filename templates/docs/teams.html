Teams

Teams in Project visualizer make collaboration seamless by bringing members together under a shared space. TEams help you assign roles, track contributions, and manage visibility with ease.

Creating & visualizing teams

To create a team, simply provide a team name and description. After creation, you can customizte your team by adding a team icon from the settings button.

Each team has a contribution chart, giving you a quick overview of member activity and progress.

Note: Visualization will be further enhanced in upcoming updates for a smoother experience.

Team visibility

  • Private: Only team members can view and access the team.
  • Public: The team can be discoverd by others, allowing wider visibility and potential new member requests.

Members & roles

You can join members directly via email. Users can also send join requests to a public team. All pending requests appear in the User management section, accessible only by team admins.

Roles:
  • Admin: Full control over team settings, member approvals, and role management.
  • Member: Standard access to collaborate on team projects.
Future updats will introduce more granular roles for advanced control.
